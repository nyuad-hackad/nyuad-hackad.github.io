HackAD 2016 Hackathon

November 30th, 2016
UNIX Lab (Next to the Library)

The Saadiyat Campus of NYUAD has only existed for 3 years, and we still have a lot of inefficiencies on campus, ranging from the outdated ordering system that our dining hall uses to the notorious laundry situation that has been the source of too much grief and pain. Some changes would certainly be nice.

The HackAD hackathon will give students the chance to experience the thrill of 24-hour non-stop hacking while solving real problems that we are all familiar with on campus. Don't miss out - this is a hackathon for our own good. Click for more information and click to sign up!
