HackAD 2016 Hackathon

November 30th, 2016
UNIX Lab (Next to the Library)

The Saadiyat Campus of NYUAD has only existed for 3 years, and we still have alot of growing pains in terms of Campus Efficiency. Whether ordering from the dining hall or booking a meeting room, some things could definetly benefit from some change.

For HackAD's 2016 Hackathon, we will be focusing on the idea of hacks for campus good. Students get the chance to experience the thrill of a hackathon all while solving the "growing pains" of new campus life. Click for more information and click to sign up!
